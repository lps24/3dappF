Caffeine

How much lives in your can?

The Caffeine in Your Can


Surprisingly to most people, there’s less caffeine in a Coca-Cola can than the average serving of instant coffee or tea. The Caffeine in our drinks contributes to the overall flavour, adding a bitterness and working alongside the sugars and sweetness to provide an enjoyable taste.


Suitable for regular consumption?

There is no recommended daily upper limit for caffeine in the UK. The Food Standards Agency in the UK state that caffeinated drinks are safe to drink every day in moderation.

Caffeine might have a mild, short-term diuretic effect on people who don’t usually consume it, but studies show that this isn’t the case for those who enjoy caffeine regularly.

Keeping away from caffeine?

A can of Coca-Cola contains 32mg of caffeine, but we offer lots of caffeine-free drinks for those wanting a completely decaf option!
